Your startup will grow or die based on your Value and Growth Hypotheses.

So what the heck are they?

Value Hypothesis

The reasons as to why customers find your product valuable enough to use.


Are your customers finding your product valuable enough to pay for it?
Do they continue to use it?
Were you correct in your assumption as to why they find it valuable?


Growth Hypothesis

The reasons as to how customers discover your product and spread the word.


Is your customer base growing as expected?
How are people finding out about it?
Were you correct in your assumption as to how they found out about it?
