Glossary

  • Leap-of-Faith Assumption
  • Value Hypothesis
  • Growth Hypothesis
  • Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
  • Feedback Loop
  • Build
  • Measure
  • Learn
  • Validated Learning
  • Pivot
  • Persevere
  • Untested critical assumptions that if wrong, would single-handedly sink your startup.
  • Your ideas as to why customers will find your product valuable enough to adopt into their lives.
  • Your ideas as to how new customers discover and adopt your product.
  • Basic version of product with only enough features to satisfy early adopters. Used to test assumptions.
  • Build-Measure-Learn iteration loop based on customer feedback. Shorter loop is better.
  • Choose a problem or assumption and develop an MVP to test assumtptions.
  • Quantify customer feedback from latest MVP tests.
  • Formulate truths about your customers based on their behavior. Use these to pivot or persevere.
  • Quantifying customer behavior truths to show a startup's present and future potential.
  • After learning about customers, a decision to change product or target audience.
  • After learning about customers, a decision to continue with current product and target audience.
