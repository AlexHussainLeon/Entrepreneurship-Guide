Every startup is built on a number of
Leap-of-Faith Assumptions.

(Being wrong on any one of these assumptions could sink the whole business.)


Do you know what your Leap-of-Faith assumptions are?

(Leap-of-Faith Assumption = Untested critical assumptions that if wrong, would sink your startup.)

Examples include:
1. People are willing to buy electric cars.
2. People will post their photos to our website.
3. People will be willing to sleep in a stranger's room.


Let's find out what you are betting your entire startup on...

Find your early adopters and test these assumptions!
